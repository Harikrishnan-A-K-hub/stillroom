03 Process

How a commission works.

Slow on purpose. Four steps, roughly three to four weeks, and you're part of every one.

  1. 01

    Bring one memory

    Send us the single moment — a file, a scan, a voice note, or just tell us where it lives. We'll take it from there.

  2. 02

    We propose a form

    Within a week you receive one proposed treatment — the object we think this particular memory wants to become.

  3. 03

    The red room

    We restore, grade, typeset and compose. You watch each stage and approve the final object before anything is fixed.

  4. 04

    Kept

    You receive the finished keepsake and its archival master. It's yours outright — nothing to renew, nothing to lose.

08 Questions

Answered plainly.

What counts as a memory?

A photograph, a voice note, a letter, a video still, a ticket stub, a recipe in someone's hand. One thing, whatever form it's in.

Do you need the original?

A clear scan or file is enough to begin. If we need a better source to do it justice, we'll tell you exactly what would help.

What do I actually get?

A finished digital keepsake plus an archival master file you keep forever. No account, no subscription, no expiry — it's a made object, not a service you rent.

Is my memory private?

Completely. We never publish commissions or share names, and your source material is deleted from our systems after delivery unless you ask us to hold it.

How long does it take?

Usually three to four weeks for a Single. We'd rather be slow and right than quick and forgettable.
